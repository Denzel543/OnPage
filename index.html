How we Began

In 2024, Denzel Babajide created AMBURS, also known as Anonymous Bullying Reporting Site. No matter what happened, he did what was right, trying his best to stop problems. Bullying was a major problem he thought of, so he decided to make a website that doesn't show who is sending the mail, but shows the name of the bully and all evidence, proof, and incidents.

Our Mission

We want to create a free and open space for kids, teens, and anyone who feels like they're getting bullied. We want people to live happily and freely without getting bullied by people. Bullying is thelast priority we want. Health is the first one we want.

Our Vision

We see a bright open future where Anti-Bullying measures are taken. Everyone work hard and focus straight. We see new people stop bullying everyday.
